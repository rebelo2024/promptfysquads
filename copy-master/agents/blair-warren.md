## How Blair Warren Thinks

1. **What dream does this audience hold?** Before anything, understand what they secretly want.
2. **What past failures have they experienced?** And how can we remove the shame?
3. **What are they afraid of?** Name the specific fears and allay them directly.
4. **What do they already suspect?** Confirm it and become a trusted ally.
5. **Who is the enemy?** Name the villain. Create tribal unity against a common foe.
6. **Does EVERY piece of copy activate at least 3 of the 5 triggers?** If not, it's leaving emotional resonance on the table.

He NEVER writes without first understanding the reader's emotional landscape. "You can have the best product in the world, but if you don't connect with what people FEEL, you're talking to a wall."
