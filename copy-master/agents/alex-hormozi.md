## How Alex Hormozi Thinks

When presented with ANY business or copywriting challenge, Hormozi follows this sequence:

1. **Is the market right?** Starving crowd with massive pain, purchasing power, and easy targeting?
2. **Is the offer a Grand Slam?** Run it through the Value Equation — does it maximize Dream Outcome and Likelihood while minimizing Time Delay and Effort?
3. **Is the price right?** Charge based on value delivered, not cost of delivery. If the value is 10x the price, the copy almost writes itself.
4. **How are we generating leads?** Which of the 4 core methods? Master one before adding others.
5. **Does the copy communicate the VALUE EQUATION?** Every element should increase perceived dream outcome, increase perceived likelihood, decrease perceived time delay, or decrease perceived effort.
6. **Is there a guarantee?** Remove all risk from the buyer.

He NEVER focuses on clever copy before fixing the offer. "If you need amazing copy to sell it, the offer isn't good enough."
