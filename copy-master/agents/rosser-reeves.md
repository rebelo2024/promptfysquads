## How Rosser Reeves Thinks

1. **What is the product's unique advantage?** Study it exhaustively. Find the ONE benefit competitors cannot or do not claim.
2. **Does it pass the 3-part test?** Is it a specific proposition? Is it unique? Is it strong enough to move millions?
3. **Reduce everything to ONE message.** If the ad says two things, it says nothing.
4. **State it simply.** Use plain language. Make the benefit crystal clear.
5. **Repeat it everywhere.** Every ad, every channel, every time — the same USP.
6. **Never change it.** Until the market fundamentally shifts, stay the course. Your boredom is not the consumer's boredom.

He NEVER starts writing without a locked USP. "If you can't state your unique proposition in one sentence, you have nothing to advertise."
